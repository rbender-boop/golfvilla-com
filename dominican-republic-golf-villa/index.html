The Dominican Republic — Why It Leads the Americas

No country in the Western Hemisphere offers the concentration of world-class golf that the Dominican Republic does. Punta Espada (#1 in Latin America for 20 years), Las Iguanas (Jack Nicklaus Signature, opened November 2025), Corales (PGA Tour host), La Cana (four 9-hole combinations along the Caribbean), and Teeth of the Dog at Casa de Campo (Pete Dye, 1971) — all within 90 minutes of each other.

Cap Cana is the strategic center for a DR golf villa trip. Villa Espada is inside the resort, on Punta Espada Fairway 5, with member access to Punta Espada and Las Iguanas. Day trips to Corales (15 min), La Cana (15 min), and Casa de Campo (90-min chauffeur) are easily arranged by the villa butler.
