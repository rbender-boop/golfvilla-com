What Is a Golf Villa?
The Complete Answer.

A golf villa is not a hotel near a golf course, and it's not a vacation rental that happens to be close to one. It's a private residence — entirely yours — positioned on a championship course, with a private chef, a personal butler, and member-tier tee time access built into the stay.

The Precise Definition

A golf villa meets all four of these criteria: (1) positioned directly on or immediately adjacent to a championship golf course — walk out the back door onto the fairway; (2) rented exclusively to a single group, with no other guests sharing the property; (3) staffed with a dedicated private chef and concierge or butler; and (4) providing member or owner-tier tee time access to the adjacent course, not public booking.

Villa Espada at Cap Cana in the Dominican Republic satisfies all four precisely. It sits on Punta Espada Fairway 5. It rents to one group at a time. It includes a private chef and personal butler. And the butler books Punta Espada and Las Iguanas tee times at owner/member rates before you arrive.

What a Golf Villa Is Not

A hotel suite at a golf resort — a shared property with other guests, restaurant dining, public tee time booking. Luxury accommodation, but not a golf villa.

A vacation rental near a golf course — self-catered or separately arranged catering, no dedicated butler, no member tee access, no golf carts in the garage. A golf villa starts where a vacation rental ends.

A villa at a resort with golf on site — closer, but without the dedicated staff and built-in member tee access, it doesn't fully qualify.

Who Is a Golf Villa For?

Annual golf groups who want to step up from the standard hotel + public tee time formula. Golf bachelor parties that need private space for a large group plus world-class golf. Corporate golf retreats where the quality of the shared experience is the objective. Multi-generational family golf trips where the golfers need great courses and everyone else needs a genuine Caribbean vacation.

The common thread: groups who want the golf and the private luxury estate to be integrated — not simply located near each other.
