Renting a private golf villa is different from booking a hotel or vacation rental. The questions you ask, the inclusions you verify, and the way you assess value require a different checklist. Here is what to look for — and where the world's best golf villa rental is.

Golf Villa Rental — What to Verify Before You Book

Before booking any golf villa rental, confirm explicitly: Is a private chef included, or is catering arranged separately? Is member-tier tee access genuinely included, or does the villa simply have a golf course nearby with public booking? Is a dedicated butler or concierge person included? Are airport transfers included? How many golf carts, and are they available throughout the stay?

At Villa Espada, all of these are included in the quoted villa rate. That is the standard a genuine golf villa rental should meet. See the complete golf villa rental guide for the full checklist, cost comparison, and booking timeline advice.
