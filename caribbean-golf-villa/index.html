Caribbean Golf Villa.
The World's Best Region.

The Caribbean is the world's premier golf villa region — world-ranked courses, year-round playing conditions, private villas on the fairway, and the combination of championship golf with a genuine Caribbean stay. Villa Espada at Cap Cana sets the standard.

Why the Caribbean Leads the World in Golf Villas

No other golf region combines championship course quality, year-round playing conditions, private villa infrastructure, and genuine Caribbean lifestyle the way the Dominican Republic's northeast coast does. Cap Cana is home to Punta Espada (#1 in Latin America for nearly two decades), Las Iguanas (Jack Nicklaus Signature, opened November 2025), and Villa Espada — positioned directly on Punta Espada Fairway 5.

The weather argument is real: the Caribbean golf season is year-round. The peak window (November–April) offers trade winds, firm fast fairways, and ideal conditions. The low season (May–October) is quieter, more flexible, and still offers excellent golf at better rates.
