Villa Espada — 8 bedrooms, private chef, butler, two golf carts, and member tee time access at Punta Espada (#1 in Latin America) and Las Iguanas (Jack Nicklaus, opened Nov 2025). This is the golf villa that defines the category.

Why Cap Cana is the World's Best Golf Villa Destination

Cap Cana is unlike any other golf resort destination in the world. Inside a single private gated community on the northeastern tip of the Dominican Republic, you have two world-ranked Jack Nicklaus Signature golf courses, an 800-slip marina, private beaches, waterparks, and Villa Espada — a private 10,000+ square foot estate positioned directly on Fairway 5 of Punta Espada.

Punta Espada has been ranked the #1 resort course in Latin America and the Caribbean for over a decade. It sits along the Caribbean Sea — five of its holes play directly along the ocean. The course is visually unlike anything you have played in the Americas. In November 2025, a second Jack Nicklaus Signature course opened next door: Las Iguanas, with three oceanside holes along the rocky Caribbean shoreline and a protected iguana nature reserve woven through the layout.

For a private golf villa stay, Cap Cana has no peer in this hemisphere. Possibly in the world.

Villa Espada — The Cap Cana Golf Villa

Villa Espada sits directly on Fairway 5 of Punta Espada. Not near the course. Not adjacent to the resort. On the fairway — the infinity pool overlooks the 5th hole, and the back terrace opens directly onto the course grounds.

The villa has 8 bedrooms, each ensuite, sleeping up to 22 guests. 10,000+ square feet of living space across multiple terraces, dining areas, and living rooms purpose-built for groups who want to stay together. A private chef handles every meal. A personal butler manages tee times, excursions, restaurant bookings, and anything else your group needs. Two six-person golf carts sit in the garage, fueled and ready.

The butler books your tee times at member/owner-tier rates at both Punta Espada and Las Iguanas — the same rates available only to property owners and members within Cap Cana. For a group of 8 playing four rounds over a week-long stay, the savings versus public rack rates are substantial. And the preference on tee time availability that member access brings is, on its own, worth the villa rate.

Cap Cana Golf — Two World-Class Courses

Punta Espada is the headline. Jack Nicklaus designed this course in 2006, and it immediately became the standard-bearer for golf in the Americas. Five holes play alongside the Caribbean Sea. The fairways are immaculate. The greens are fast and precise. The visual drama on the ocean-facing holes is extraordinary. It has been ranked #1 in Latin America consistently for eighteen years.

Las Iguanas opened in November 2025 and changed the Cap Cana equation entirely. Another Nicklaus Signature design — this one incorporating a protected iguana nature reserve, caves, wetlands, and three consecutive holes directly along the Caribbean coastline at holes 12, 13, and 14. The par-three 13th is already drawing comparisons to the great oceanside par-threes of Ireland and Scotland. The course is brand new, the buzz is building, and booking the opening year of a world-class Nicklaus design is a once-in-a-career opportunity.

Your villa stay includes member access to both.

Who Stays at a Cap Cana Golf Villa

The groups who make the most of Villa Espada tend to fall into a few categories. Annual golf trip groups — typically 8 to 16 guys who have been to Scottsdale, the Outer Banks, maybe Ireland — who want to step up to something that genuinely cannot be replicated in the US. They've heard about Cap Cana from someone who's been. They book a week and never stop talking about it.

Golf bachelor parties are another strong fit. The villa accommodates up to 22 guests, and the combination of championship golf, private pool, private chef cooking every meal, and a location that is legitimately world-class creates the conditions for the best golf bachelor party anywhere in the world.

Corporate golf retreats work exceptionally well. Clients who have traveled widely and are difficult to impress. A private villa on a Nicklaus course they've never played, a chef handling everything, a butler arranging the tee sheet — it registers differently than a resort hotel stay.

Cap Cana Golf Villa Rates

Villa Espada rates are $2,500/night (May–October low season), $4,500/night (November–April peak season), and $6,500/night for Christmas, New Year's, and Easter. All rates include private chef, butler, housekeeping, two golf carts, member tee time access at Punta Espada and Las Iguanas, and private airport transfers from Punta Cana International.

The minimum stay is 3 nights in low season and 5 nights in peak season. All rates are subject to 18% Dominican Republic government tax. Green fees are billed at member/owner tier rates, separately, at the time of play.

For a group of 8 splitting the villa, peak season pricing works out to $562.50 per person per night — all-inclusive for accommodation, staff, and golf cart access. The per-person economics of a villa stay versus eight hotel rooms plus eight restaurant meals plus eight separate transfers are almost always more favorable once you run them.
