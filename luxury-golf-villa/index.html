Luxury Golf Villa

True luxury in a golf villa is specific. It's measured in the caliber of the course, the quality of the private staff, the positioning of the villa on the fairway, and the seamlessness with which every logistical detail is managed. Villa Espada at Cap Cana defines the category.

What Luxury Actually Means in a Golf Villa

A luxury golf villa requires four things: (1) a world-ranked championship course directly adjacent — not a resort-quality layout that happens to be nearby; (2) the villa positioned on the course, not simply within a resort that has golf; (3) dedicated private staff with a private chef and personal butler included in the rate; and (4) member-tier tee access pre-booked before you arrive.

On all four criteria, Villa Espada is the standard. Punta Espada — #1 in Latin America for nearly two decades. Positioned on Fairway 5. Private chef and butler included. Member tee times at Punta Espada and Las Iguanas pre-booked before you land.
