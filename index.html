Golf Villa · Defined

Not Near a Golf Course.
On One.

A golf villa isn't a hotel near a resort course. A true golf villa is a private residence — your own house — positioned on or directly adjacent to a championship golf course. Your group has it entirely. The chef cooks for you. The butler books your tee times at member rates. Two golf carts sit in the garage, fueled.

You wake up, walk out the back door, and you are on the fairway. No lobby. No shared pool. No strangers. Just your group, an extraordinary course, and every logistical detail handled before you arrived.

That's what a golf villa is. GolfVilla.com exists to help you find the right one — and book it right.

Golf Villa vs. Golf Resort Hotel

Why a Villa
Changes Everything.

A golf resort hotel gives you a room and a tee time. A golf villa gives you a private home, a chef who remembers you take your coffee black, a butler with every tee time pre-booked, and the ability to walk to the first tee in your socks.

For groups — golf bachelor parties, annual golf trips, corporate outings, family reunions — the villa is almost always the better financial decision once you run the numbers. One all-inclusive rate per night. Split eight ways. Compared to eight hotel rooms, eight restaurant dinners, eight round-trip transfers.

The math changes the answer. And the experience isn't comparable.
