Corporate Golf Villa Retreat.
Total Privacy. World-Class Golf.

Villa Espada at Cap Cana is the format for corporate golf entertaining that impresses clients who have seen everything. A private estate on Punta Espada Fairway 5, dedicated chef and butler, the #1-ranked course in Latin America, and complete privacy from arrival to departure.

A corporate golf retreat at a private villa on a world-ranked course delivers what a resort hotel cannot: total privacy for every client interaction, dedicated hospitality staff focused exclusively on your group, and a quality signal that clients who have traveled extensively immediately recognise as exceptional. Villa Espada at Cap Cana is the property that makes this format real — on Punta Espada Fairway 5, the Jack Nicklaus Signature course ranked #1 in Latin America and the Caribbean.

Why a Golf Villa Outperforms a Resort Hotel for Corporate Entertaining

Privacy is total. At a resort hotel, your clients are in a shared environment — lobby encounters with other guests, restaurant conversations that are overheard, golf starter sheets that other groups also use. None of this applies at Villa Espada. The property is exclusively yours. Your clients are the only people on it. Every conversation, every meal, every moment of the experience is private.

The quality signal is immediate. Clients who have traveled to Scottsdale, Pebble Beach, or Scotland land in Cap Cana, transfer through the gates of a private gated resort, and arrive at a private villa on the fairway of the #1-ranked course in Latin America. They understand immediately that this trip is different from every other corporate golf experience they've had. That impression is set before anyone picks up a club.

Staff are dedicated to your group alone. A resort concierge handles requests from dozens or hundreds of guests simultaneously. The Villa Espada butler works exclusively for your group. When a client wants something — a specific bottle of wine at dinner, an earlier tee time, a request for their particular dietary requirements at every meal — it is handled immediately and without the friction of a shared hospitality operation.

The golf is genuinely world-class. Punta Espada is ranked #1 in Latin America by GolfWeek. It has hosted the PGA Champions Tour. Nine holes play along the Caribbean Sea. Your clients are not playing a good resort course — they are playing one of the best courses in the Americas, at owner rates, with tee times arranged before they arrive. That's a different conversation at dinner than "we played the resort course."

Villa Espada for Corporate Groups

Property and Capacity

8 bedrooms, up to 22 guests. Multiple private dining and living spaces within the villa allow for both large-group meals and smaller breakout conversations. The outdoor terrace overlooking Punta Espada Fairway 5 is the natural setting for evening client entertaining — a fairway and Caribbean sky as the backdrop to a private chef-prepared dinner.

The Butler

For a corporate retreat, the butler function is critical: the entire logistics of the trip are managed by one person whose sole focus is your group. Tee times confirmed before arrival. Airport transfers coordinated for all incoming flights. Special requests handled without friction. Off-property dinner reservations at the St. Regis Cap Cana if desired. The butler removes every operational variable from your event — you focus on the clients, the butler handles the rest.

The Chef

Every meal is a private client entertainment opportunity. The chef prepares breakfast, lunch, and dinner for the full group, daily, to a standard that positions your hospitality at the top of what clients have experienced. Dietary requirements are accommodated without explanation. Pre-arrival menu consultation is standard. There are no restaurant reservations, no group meal logistics, no splitting across tables. Every meal is set for your full group in a private dining environment.

The Golf

Member-rate tee times at Punta Espada and Las Iguanas, pre-booked. Two six-person golf carts from the villa garage to the first tee. The butler arranges day trips to Teeth of the Dog at Casa de Campo if the group wants to add a third course to the agenda. For clients who play, the courses speak for themselves. For clients who don't play — beach access at Juanillo, the St. Regis spa, the Cap Cana Marina, and Scape Park are all available through the butler.

Sample Corporate Golf Retreat — 4 Nights

  • Day 1 — VIP Arrivals. Butler coordinates all incoming transfers from PUJ. Private welcome at the villa. Chef prepares a formal welcome dinner on the terrace. Tee sheet confirmed for the week.
  • Day 2 — Punta Espada. Morning tee time at Punta Espada at member rates. Lunch at the villa. Afternoon at leisure — pool, beach club, or spa arranged through butler. Private dinner.
  • Day 3 — Las Iguanas. Morning round at Las Iguanas — the new Nicklaus Signature course, three oceanside holes. Afternoon: Juanillo Beach or St. Regis spa option. Signature client dinner, terrace, chef's preparation.
  • Day 4 — Final Round + Departure. Morning round at Punta Espada. Lunch. Private departures throughout afternoon, butler-coordinated transfers to PUJ.
